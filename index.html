What Exactly is a Volume Booster?

A Volume Booster extension is far from an ordinary browser extension; it's a must-have for anyone seeking an enhanced and robust audio experience. Besides, this tool enables users to surpass the constraints of their speakers or headphones, delivering a volume boost well beyond the standard limits. Picture a video or music track playing at a barely audible level. Furthermore, with just one click, Volume Booster amplifies the sound, ensuring every beat and note resonates loudly and clearly.

  • Get the Volume Booster Extension First, ensure your device has the Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Microsoft Edge browser. Then, install the extension on your Windows PC, Chromebook, Mac, or any compatible computer.
  • Attach the Extension to Your Toolbar Once you've downloaded the extension, pin it to your browser's toolbar. Further, click on the icon next to your address bar, then select the "TP" icon to proceed.
  • Sign In to Your Preferred Streaming Account Next, select your favorite streaming service—Netflix, YouTube, HBO Max, Disney Plus Hotstar, Crunchyroll, Amazon Prime Video, Hulu, Paramount Plus, Peacock TV, JioCinema, or Fancode. Then, log in by entering all the necessary details completely.
  • Find, Choose, Play, and Pause Afterwards, look for the video you want to watch with your friends. Then, select and play the video, pausing it for a few moments to generate a link for joining the Volume Booster Chrome extension.
  • Host a Volume Booster Party Next, click on the “TP” icon next to your address bar. A new “Create A Party” window will open. Then, click the “Start a Party” button to generate an instant watch party URL. Copy this link and share it with your friends. During setup, you can choose whether to keep control of the party by enabling the “Only I have Control” option, which will turn green. If you prefer not to control the party, turn off this option.
  • Enter the Volume Booster Party To join the Volume Booster Party, click on the provided party URL, which will direct you straight to the Volume Booster. Then, click the “TP” icon in the address bar to join the party.
